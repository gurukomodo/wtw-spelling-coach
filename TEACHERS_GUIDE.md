# UnBoxEd Spelling Coach: Teacher’s Guide

Welcome to the UnBoxEd Spelling Coach. This application is designed to move you from "grading papers" to "coaching students" by leveraging AI to handle the heavy lifting of linguistic analysis.

## Core Features & Classroom Benefits

### 1. Dynamic Assessment Cycles
**Feature:** Instead of a static "one-and-done" test, the system supports dynamic assessment templates and a bi-weekly cycle (tracked in `constants.py`).
**Teacher Benefit:** 
*   **Continuous Growth Tracking:** Move away from summative "final exams" and toward **formative assessment**. By testing every 14 days, you can see exactly when a student masters a concept and when they plateau, allowing for real-time adjustments to your teaching plan.
*   **Fresh Material:** Use different test templates to ensure students are applying their knowledge to new words, preventing rote memorization.

### 2. G-Level Diagnostic Mappings (g0–g8)
**Feature:** Every spelling attempt is automatically mapped against nine specific linguistic groups—from Phonemic Awareness (g0) to Morphemic Reduction (g8).
**Teacher Benefit:**
*   **Instant Differentiation:** No more manual "feature counting." The AI identifies the student's **Learning Frontier**—the exact point where their phonological or orthographic knowledge breaks down.
*   **Targeted Small Groups:** Easily see which students are struggling with the same specific feature (e.g., "Silent-e" or "Consonant Blends") to form highly effective, differentiated instruction groups in seconds.
*   **ESL-Aware Insights:** The system specifically looks for Mandarin L1 transfer patterns (like final consonant deletion or /l/ vs /r/ confusion), giving you insights that a standard spelling test would miss.

### 3. AI Handwriting Transcription
**Feature:** Upload a photo of student work, and the AI transcribes it into a digital `intended:attempt` format.
**Teacher Benefit:**
*   **Massive Time Savings:** Eliminate the "data entry" phase of assessment. By automating the transcription, you save hours of typing, allowing you to spend that time providing direct feedback or planning your next lesson.
*   **Digital Portfolios:** Every assessment is stored in a searchable history, creating a permanent digital record of student progress that can be easily shared during parent-teacher conferences.

## The Workflow: Your 5-Step Path to Insight

1.  **Capture:** Snap a photo of the student's spelling sheet.
2.  **Transcribe:** Let the AI read the handwriting (you stay in control by verifying the text).
3.  **Analyze:** The AI Crew analyzes the phonemes and graphemes, comparing them against the G-Level rules.
4.  **Refine:** Add your own "Gold Standard" observations in the Teacher Refinement box.
5.  **Action:** Use the AI-generated **Personalized Practice Lists** to give each student exactly the 10 words they need to study next.
